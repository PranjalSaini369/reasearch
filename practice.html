What is IQ Testing?

IQ (Intelligence Quotient) tests measure a range of cognitive abilities and provide a score that is intended to serve as a measure of an individual's intellectual abilities and potential.

While not a complete measure of intelligence, IQ tests can be valuable tools for understanding cognitive strengths and areas for improvement.

Recommended IQ Test Resources:

  • Mensa International
  • Official IQ Test
  • 123Test
  • Free-IQTest
  • Culture Fair IQ Test

Quick IQ Assessment

Try this sample question to test your logical reasoning:

If all Bloops are Razzies and some Razzies are Tubbies, then which of the following must be true?

A. All Tubbies are Bloops
B. Some Bloops are Tubbies
C. No Tubbies are Bloops
D. None of the above
Correct!

The correct answer is D. None of the above. The statements don't provide enough information to support any of the other conclusions.
